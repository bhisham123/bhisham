About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Department of Computer Science at Wake Forest University. I earned my Ph.D. from the School of Mathematics and Statistical Sciences at the Indian Institute of Technology Mandi, Himachal Pradesh, India. My research interests broadly focus on randomized dimension reduction, numerical linear algebra, machine learning, optimization, and soft computing.

The broader areas of my research interest are Scalable Algorithms for Data Science, Dimension Reduction/Sketching Algorithms, and Machine Learning. Currently, I am working on sketching algorithms for dimension reduction. More specifically, I am exploring the areas of developing new dimensionality reduction algorithms for different data types and the underlying similarity measures. Another related direction I am working on is improving existing sketching algorithms.
